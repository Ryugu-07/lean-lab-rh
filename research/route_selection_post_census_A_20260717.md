## Why H6-B

H6-B has zero direct RH-frontier delta, but it is the mandatory M0-style bridge before any
de Bruijn-Newman proof attempt can make a statement about `Mathlib.RiemannHypothesis`. It also has
a decisive failure mode: either the exact source normalization compiles, or the missing
theta-Mellin theorem is isolated at a fixed statement. This gives more route information than
another generic zero-count wrapper.

The selection does not prohibit direct RH, W2/G7, or M2/G3 work. H10 Bombieri-Stepanov census work
continues as a parallel literature recommendation, not as the selected proof campaign.
