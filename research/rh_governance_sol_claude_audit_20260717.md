## Joint audit conclusion retained

- The reviewed Lean output is mathematically real at the level claimed: the audited criterion
  equivalences compile, the selected axiom audit contains no project-specific axioms, and the
  vendored source scan reports no proof placeholders.
- Progress accounting had become too easy to inflate by splitting one route into many local
  subedges. Some `BRIDGE_REDUCED` entries describe route infrastructure, not a reduction of the
  unconditional RH frontier.
- Both facts coexist: kernel-checked theorems remain assets, while their semantic classification
  must be corrected when the ledger overstates what changed.

## V4.1 resolution

The remedy is semantic, not numerical:

- preregister a fixed mathematical endpoint and known obstruction;
- classify infrastructure, known-theorem formalization, falsification, and open-frontier progress
  separately;
- require theorem, witness, TargetChecks, axiom audit, and definition alignment for claims;
- use periodic user/Sol/Claude review for public statements;
- select work by route/DAG/conjecture value rather than by the number of loops or commits.

The V4 proof freeze, route/week budgets, commit/line fuses, paper-review percentage, W2
preapproval, and M2/G3 closure do not survive V4.1. They have no current force.

## Exposure findings retained

This package remains the first priority and can run in parallel with `PROOF-ATTEMPT` work. Its
P1/P2/P3 states govern release language, not permission to research.
