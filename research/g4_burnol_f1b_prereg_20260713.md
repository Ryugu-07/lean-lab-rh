## Fixed Normalization And Signs

For the project Fourier frequency `xi : Real`, set

```text
s(xi) = 1/2 + (2*pi*xi) I,
m(xi) = (s(xi)-1)/s(xi).
```

The forward multiplier is `m`; the inverse multiplier is `conj(m)`. On the critical line,
`|m(xi)|=1`. After conjugation by `baezDuarteFourierMellinL2`, the resulting positive-half-line
operator is Burnol's `T=(1-M)^(-1)` and must satisfy

```text
T chi = chi1,
T (rho(theta/t)) = -A(t/theta).
```

Here `chi1(t)=(1+log t) chi_(0,1](t)`. The pointwise minus sign in the kernel identity is required,
although it disappears after taking the complex span.
